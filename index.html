questions and answers

What types of services do you offer?

We provide a wide array of services, including plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, painting, tiling, general home repairs, installations, and much more. Whether it's a small fix or a large renovation project, we've got you covered.

How quickly can I expect a response to my service request?

We understand the urgency of home repairs. We strive to respond to all service requests within 24 hours. Our team will work with you to schedule a suitable appointment at your earliest convenience.

Are your handymen licensed and insured?

Yes, all of our handymen are licensed professionals with extensive experience in their respective trades. Additionally, we are fully insured, ensuring that both our team and your property are protected throughout the service.

What sets your services apart from others?

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction makes us stand out. We prioritize using high-quality materials, transparent pricing, and delivering excellent workmanship on every project, regardless of its size.

How do you determine the cost for a project?

We offer a free initial consultation to assess the scope of the work needed. Our pricing is transparent, and we provide detailed estimates based on the project requirements and materials involved. We aim to ensure fair and competitive pricing for all our services.

What areas do you serve?

We provide our services across Thane. Our coverage area extends to a 50-mile radius, catering to homeowners and businesses in this region.
